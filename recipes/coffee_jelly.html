Coffee Jelly Recipe

Description

Coffee Jelly is a delicious coffee-flavored cubed gelatin in a thick coffee-infused sweetened cream. A great dessert for coffee lovers! It will surely satisfy your love for coffee.

The combination of coffee, agar, and cream makes coffee jelly an extraordinarily simple dessert. One of the easiest and most delicious desserts you can make. Another refreshing treat!

Ingredients

  • 25 g unflavored gelatin
  • 5 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 tbsp instant coffee powder
  • 1 can 12 oz evaporated milk
  • 3/4 can of 14 oz condensed milk
  • 1 cup whipping cream or all purpose cream
  • 1 tsp instant coffee powder
  • cooked sago (tapioca pears), optional

Steps

  1. In a large sauce pan, combine gelatin, water and sugar. Stir until sugar and gelatin is dissolved.
  2. Stir-in coffee 2 1/2 tbsp coffee powder.
  3. Turn on the stove to medium heat. Stir occasionally until mixture starts to boil. Turn off heat and transfer mixture into heated proof containers. Let it cool completely before slicing into cubes.
  4. In large bowl, combine evaporated milk, condensed milk, whipping cream or all purpose cream and 1 tsp coffee powder. Stir until well combined.
  5. Add the cubed coffee flavored gelatin and stir to combine.
  6. Add cooked sago (tapioca pearls), if desired.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
